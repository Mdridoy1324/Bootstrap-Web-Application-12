Organic vs. Non-Organic Food

Aspect Organic Food Non-Organic Food
Production Methods Uses natural fertilizers and pesticides, avoids synthetic chemicals Uses synthetic fertilizers and pesticides
Growth Hormones Prohibited May be used
Antibiotics Restricted use May be used
Genetic Modification Prohibited May be genetically modified
Environmental Impact Emphasizes sustainability, biodiversity, and soil health May contribute to pollution and soil degradation
Health Benefits Lower pesticide residues, potentially higher nutrient content May contain pesticide residues
